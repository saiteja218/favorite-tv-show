The Family man

Synopsis: "Family Man" is an Indian action thriller web series created by Raj and DK. The series follows the life of Srikant Tiwari, a middle-class man with a secret double life as a spy for the Threat Analysis and Surveillance Cell (TASC), a fictional intelligence agency. Balancing his duties as a family man with the high-stakes world of espionage, Srikant finds himself entangled in dangerous missions while trying to protect his loved ones from the perils of his covert profession. As he navigates through complex situations, Srikant must confront moral dilemmas and make tough choices that have far-reaching consequences for both his personal and professional life. With its gripping storyline, intense action sequences, and compelling characters, "Family Man" explores themes of duty, sacrifice, and the blurred lines between right and wrong in the pursuit of justice.
