True Detective

Synopsis: "True Detective" Season 1 follows detectives Rust Cohle and Martin Hart as they investigate a grisly murder case in Louisiana, spanning seventeen years. As they delve deeper, they uncover a tangled web of corruption and personal demons. The season explores themes of morality and existentialism while featuring masterful performances by Matthew McConaughey and Woody Harrelson.
